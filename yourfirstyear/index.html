How it works

1. The wedding & You

Tell us a little bit about you, the newlyweds and their wedding, so we can set everything up.

2. The gifts

Think of a gift for the newlyweds for each month of their first wedding year. The more personal, the better it (usually) is!

Need inspiration? Take them out for ice cream during a hot summer day! Go bobsledding together on a cozy winter night! Gift them a relaxing massage at a nearby spa!

3. The message

Write a short message to the Newlyweds for each month. And you're done!

Make them smile! Share a fun anecdote why the gift fits them. Reminisce about their perfect wedding day. Share with them how you did this activity and why they should look forward to it!

4. The delivery

We take care of the rest. On the day after their wedding, we send the Newlyweds an e-mail congratulating and asking them to confirm their e-mail address. Then, every month on the day of their wedding, we will e-mail them your message and gift!

If you do not want to turn up empty handed at the wedding, why not frame a picture of you with them and explain what you got them in a card?
